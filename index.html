Intel Innovation Timeline

Explore Intel's journey through decades of technological innovation. This interactive timeline showcases the milestones that shaped the future of computing and semiconductor technology.

Our Journey

1968

Intel Corporation is founded, laying the foundation for decades of technological innovation.

1971

The Intel 4004, the world's first commercial microprocessor, is introduced to the market.

1978

Launch of the Intel 8086 processor, establishing the x86 architecture standard.

1985

Intel 386 processor introduces 32-bit architecture, enabling new computing capabilities.

1993

Pentium processor launches, revolutionizing personal computing with advanced performance.

2006

Intel Core processors debut, setting new standards for multi-core computing efficiency.

2015

Intel releases Skylake architecture, advancing computing performance to new heights.

2021

Intel commits to process technology leadership with next-generation manufacturing innovation.

2024

Intel continues shaping the future with advanced semiconductor solutions and sustainable practices.
